Year of Experience

Empowering , Developing and Promoting all Cue-Sports Nationally

KPBF was formed in 2022 and is officially registered with the registrar of sports on line with the 2010 Sports Act.The board is comprised of mostly fellow pool players from past to present. Pool is arguably the most played adult sport in the country ,boasting over 10,000 pool tables and over half a million regular players.

Over the years Kenya has partaken in various regional and international pool championships. Our 1st stint at the World Eightball Pool Championships was in 2003 , in Blackpool England. We sent teams to this event 5 times to date , in the years 2004,2006,2008 and 2012.

Our regional championships are the 2014 , 2016 , 2022 and 2025 all africa pool championships.Lately we have also sent players to China for heyball tournaments. Heyball is a slightly different version of pool played on the bigger 9 foot tables n started by the Chinese. It's popularity worldwide is growing very fast. We recently sent players to the Heyball Commonwealth games held in Mauritius.KPBF is affiliated to the All Africa Pool Association ( AAPA ) and by extension to the World Pool Association ( WPA ).
